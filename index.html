Mission Briefing

The SentiChain ecosystem is built in layers. Each layer strengthens the foundation of sentiment data, refines it into insights, and culminates in investible foresight.

How It Works

Vectorize

Transformer models turn posts into dense matrices, then sign them for authenticity.

Verify on Chain

Signed matrices become immutable transactions stored with a Merkle-based Consensus Root.

Query the API

Retrieve sentiment and market correlations via our RESTful endpoints.

Aparecium

Experience reversible embeddings that reveal text from vectors while keeping data private.
